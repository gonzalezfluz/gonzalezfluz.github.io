OUR VISION

Within our lifetime, all individuals around the world will have access to a widely recognized and accepted international digital financial identity and financial health score as well as the information, resources, tools and support necessary to be active participants and creators of the world economy.

.

"Houston, we have a problem..."

In the United States, there are 70 million people
who are underbanked or unbanked.

Traditional finance does not work for millions of people in the U.S. and around the world. Credit scores don't work across borders.

KONVIV is here to change that.

A globally accesible financial identity for everyone on the planet

Konviv is a mobile startup that helps individuals create a digital financial identity while budgeting, tracking, managing and growing their wealth by providing the necessary information, tools, resources, and support to help them reach financial freedom. Konviv users will be able to receive incremental loans through our peer-to-peer marketplace based on their Konviv score and get help paying it back through AI-driven notifications and "smart buckets" that automatically adjust your spending and savings. As you grow, Konviv learns your financial behavior and provides you customized financial guidance.

We are currently building an Android version and SMS analog version of our application.

Konviv is a data-driven mobile platform that provides smart budgeting tools, financial literacy, peer-2-peer lending, and personal support for the underbanked.


GLOBAL FINANCIAL IDENTITY AND SCORE

Through the continued use of Konviv, individuals will create and inform their Konviv score, an alternative credit score. This will serve to establish a global digital financial identity that will allow users to access increasing financial opportunities all over the world, including credit.

BUDGET, TRACK, MANAGE ASSETS

Konviv will allow users to budget, track, manage and grow their wealth. Through our platform, individuals and businesses will be able to track and trade money in multiple currencies, commodities (raw goods), and other types of assets.

PEER-2-PEER LENDING: ACCESS TO CAPITAL

Konviv users will be able to receive incremental loans through our peer-to-peer marketplace based on their Konviv score. The P2P marketplace allows individuals on our platform to lend each other small amounts of money for interest or a flat fee.

Mobile financial solutions are gaining traction around the world.

In Kenya, more than 72% of people living on less than $1.25 a day are now using M-Pesa, a mobile money system.

Today, more than 43% of Kenya's GDP flows through M-Pesa with over 237 million peer-to-peer transactions.

Imagine the possibilities around the corner.

1 billion people around the world have access to mobile phones and that number is growing rapidly. We're at the beginning of a tremendous opportunity to disrupt traditional finance while providing billions of people an opportunity to access new forms of capital and grow their income through new digital tools, opportunities, and information.

First Nations Communities Pilot

Konviv has the privilege to work with various First Nations on a pilot involving various communities, including the Apache, Seneca, and Lakota. Tribal leaders from these three communities have given Konviv their go ahead to launch the product in these communities. We are honored and humbled.

The Konviv pilot will serve to create a financial platform that community members can use to engage in trade, manage their assets, and gain access to capital that has long been denied due to many individuals not having access to or wanting traditional credit.

Our Story

Born in Monterrey, Mexico, Luz moved to the U.S. with her family at a young age. Like many who immigrated to the U.S. from distressed regions of the world, Luz remembers growing up on food stamps and standing in line at salvation army to receive gifts during the holidays. Luz was determined to change her circumstances and graduated from U.C. Berkeley with an undergraduate degree in political economy and then obtained a Juris Doctorate in Law. Luz's name means light, and she knows full well the challenges that many immigrants face as they teach themselves to change their circumstances.

Deep was born in Patna, India and moved to the U.S. at the age of one. As a child of immigrants from a part of the world that has been impoverished for decades, Deep understands the difficult imprint that this situation leaves for many generations. He grew up understanding the privilege of being the first in his family to live outside these extreme circumstances. Like many in Calcutta, Deep's father grew up without electricity and would spend hours studying under the flame of a single candle so he could create a better future for himself and his family. Deep means candlelight.

Deep and Luz are committed to bringing more light to the world through their work with Konviv.

The meaning of KONVIV

Konviv comes from the Spanish word: convivir. Convivir is a beautiful, poetic, and lively word. It literally means to coexist or to exist side by side with something. Convivir acknowledges that we are all here together and part of the same story and trajectory. It urges us to think of the world, our actions, and our work as more than our own, it is ours.

Konviv is an acknowledgement that finances are an undeniable part of our social fabric. With every financial transaction there is a human exchange. Konviv strives to make each financial exchange more touching, more empowering, and more impactful. Konviv - more than a financial app - is a movement.
